Protein Regions
======================================================================

.. card:: Description

     
            With this panel you can ...

            - zoom a selection of atoms.
        




Getting Started
---------------

Show an atom selection as sticks on the Proteins tab
****************************************************
1. Select a protein from the tree.
2. Click on |open_in_new_w200|, to load the pymol session of the selected protein.
3. Click on the atoms in PyMOL's sequence view that should be zoomed into.
4. Under the *Hotspots* menu, click on **Protein Regions**.



Details
-------
- The zoom is fixed at a certain distance from the selection. If the protein is small, the zoom may be too small.
