Hotspots

Here you can display specific hotspots of a selected protein.

To display a hotspot, click on a protein or protein pair name of the list.

Define hotspot region

To define the hotspot region, click on the residues in PyMOLs protein sequence view.

Show residues as sticks

To display the selected hotspot as balls and sticks, click on Show (Residues as sticks).

Hide residues as sticks

To hide the balls and sticks representation of the selected hotspot, click on Hide (Residues as sticks).

Zoom to hotspot

To zoom to the selected hotspot, click on Zoom (Residue position).
