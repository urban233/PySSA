Results

Here you can see the results from a protein pair.

You can choose the protein pair using the combo box.

It displays the root-mean squared deviation of the structure alignment.


Color (By RMSD)

Click to color the protein based on the RMSD value.


No. of aligned residues

It displays the number of residues which were aligned during the structure alignment.


View (Distance Plot)

Click to open the distance plot, which is a x-y plot of the residue pair no. and distances.


View (Distance Histogram)

Click to open the distance histogram, which shows the frequency of certain distance intervals.


View (Distance Table)

Click to open the distance table, which shows a detailed table of the analysis.


View (Structure alignment)

Click to open the image of the structure alignment.


View (Interesting regions)

Click to open the image of the selected interesting region.
